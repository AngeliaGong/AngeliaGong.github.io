About Me

Welcome! My name is Angelia, and I am a software developer and a student pursuing Honours Bachelor of Computer Science degree in University of Waterloo.

In my first two work terms , I co-developed a conversation bot for Dell using C# .NET frame work and Microsoft LUIS. It reports data from enterprise communication platforms and significantly eases the process of human resource management for client companies. I also developed various Data-mining APIs for the national and provincial student loans websites using C# when I worked in D+H.

I enjoyed both of my working experiences, and had been very excited to learn new technologies from colleagues.

I define my self not only a passionate developer, but also an optimistic challenge lover.

I love learning and trying a variety of activities. I learned Latin dance and Jazz dance at a young age. I can play flute and have just started learning guitar. I love roller-coasters, bungee jumping, and am looking forward to try sky-diving by next year.

My strongest passion besides computer science is travelling. I have been saving money just to travel since my first work term. I have planed and organized trips to Cuba, Yellowknife, Tokyo and Morocco.
